# King-Fahd-Medical-City-EDA
Explorative data analysis from the ministry of health King-Fahd-Medical-City.
DATA DICTIONARY
- ID number
- KFMCFN: Patient's file number at King Fahd Medical City
- Full Name
- AGE
- BREAST DENSITY according to:
• a: the breasts are almost entirely fatty
• b: there are scattered areas of fibro-glandular density
• c: the breasts are heterogeneously dense, which may obscure small masses
• d: the breasts are extremely dense, which lowers the sensitivity of mammography

BIRADS: MAMMO BIRADS according to:

- US: ultrasound BI-RADS
- BI-RADS score of 0: requires additional tests and images to provide a final assessment
- BI-RADS score of 1: negative
- BI-RADS score of 2 benign
- BI-RADS score of 3 probably benign
- BI-RADS score of 4 suspicious
- BI-RADS score of 5 high suspicions of cancer

- CALCIFICATION: a “marker” for some underlying process that is occurring in the breast tissue. In
most cases, the process is benign (not associated with cancer) or suspicious

- C. TYPE: cancer type
- STAGE: T: tumor, N: Lymph node, M: metastasis
- GRADE:
1: usually means the cancer is slower-growing and less likely to spread.
2: means the cancer is growing faster than a grade 1 cancer but slower than a grade 3 cancer
3: means a faster-growing cancer that’s more likely to spread.
- MARKERS: breast cancer hormone receptors status (ER,PR,H)
- US SIZE: size of tumor by ultrasound
- SURGERY SIZE
- LN: LYMPH NODE
- MEATASTASES
- L.CTOMY: Lumpectomy

- M. CTOMY: mastectomy
- SLNB: SENTINAL LYMPH NODE BIOPSY
